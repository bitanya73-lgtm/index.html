✨ Latest Club News & Updates ✨

🏆 Youth Academy Wins National Championship!

May 25, 2025

Our promising young talents have done it! The Bahir Dar Kenema FC Youth Academy U17 squad clinched the Ethiopian Youth National Championship title in a thrilling final match against Adama City FC's youth team, winning 2-1 after extra time. This victory highlights the strength of our youth development program and promises a bright future for the senior team. Celebrations are planned for next week at the stadium.

🤝 Partnership with Local Charity "Hope for Youth" Announced

May 20, 2025

Bahir Dar Kenema FC is proud to announce a new strategic partnership with local charity "Hope for Youth". This collaboration aims to provide sports equipment, coaching, and educational support to underprivileged children in our community. As part of the initiative, several first-team players will visit local schools and conduct training sessions. We believe in giving back to the community that supports us.

📈 Coach's Corner: Analyzing Our Performance in the Premier League

May 18, 2025

Head Coach Ato Getachew discusses the team's recent form and upcoming challenges in the Ethiopian Premier League. "We've shown great resilience in recent matches, especially the away draw against Hawassa City SC. Our focus remains on consistency and improving our attacking third efficiency," Getachew stated. He emphasized the importance of fan support for the final stretch of the season.

🚑 Injury Update: Captain Mesfin Tadesse Recovering Well

May 16, 2025

Good news for Bahir Dar Kenema FC fans! Club captain Mesfin Tadesse, who sustained a hamstring injury three weeks ago, is making excellent progress in his recovery. The medical team anticipates his return to full training within the next two weeks. His leadership and experience will be a significant boost for the crucial upcoming fixtures.

🏟️ Stadium Renovation Plans Underway

May 10, 2025

Exciting times ahead for our home ground! Initial plans for the renovation and expansion of Bahir Dar Stadium have been approved. The project aims to enhance spectator experience, improve training facilities, and meet international standards. Further details on the phases of construction and expected completion dates will be shared soon.

🌟 Player Spotlight: Birhanu Bekele's Rising Star

Forward Birhanu Bekele has been a revelation this season, with his dazzling dribbles and crucial goals making him a fan favorite.

Our Story: The Rise of the Walias

Founded in 2009, Bahir Dar Kenema Football Club, affectionately known as "The Walias" (after Ethiopia's national animal, a symbol of resilience and pride), is more than just a football team; it's a heartbeat of the vibrant city of Bahir Dar. From humble beginnings, we've rapidly ascended through the Ethiopian football landscape, cementing our place as a formidable force in the Ethiopian Premier League. Our journey is a testament to unwavering dedication, passionate support, and a commitment to excellence on and off the pitch.

Our vibrant colors of green and yellow aren't just an aesthetic choice; they embody the lush landscapes of our home region, the promise of growth, and the golden aspirations of our community. We've proudly reached national cup finals and consistently challenged for top honors, captivating fans with our dynamic and attractive style of play.

Our Vision: Beyond the Pitch

Bahir Dar Kenema is driven by a profound mission: to be a catalyst for football development throughout Ethiopia. We are deeply committed to nurturing young talent through robust youth programs, fostering a deep sense of community pride, and elevating Ethiopian football to new heights on the continental stage. Our core values of integrity, teamwork, and relentless pursuit of excellence are the cornerstones of everything we do.

Community: The Heart of the Walias

Our connection with the community is paramount. Bahir Dar Kenema actively engages in a wide array of outreach initiatives, including dynamic youth training academies that inspire future generations, impactful social development projects that address local needs, and vibrant participation in local events. These programs are designed not just to unearth the next footballing talents but also to promote healthy lifestyles and instill positive values within the region.

Our Home: Bahir Dar Stadium

Feel the roar of the crowd and the electric atmosphere as the Walias take to the field at Bahir Dar Stadium! This modern and impressive facility, with a seating capacity of over 60,000, provides an unparalleled match-day experience for our loyal supporters. It's more than just a stadium; it's our fortress, where history is made and dreams are pursued.
